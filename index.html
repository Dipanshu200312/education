Welcome to AAFM® India

AAFM® India is the Indian chapter of AAFM® USA and offers globally recognized certifications in finance, wealth management, estate planning, financial analysis, and economics.

Our Journey

Established in 2011, AAFM® India, the official chapter of AAFM® USA, has evolved over the years to lead in wealth management & finance education. With NISM-accredited regulatory training, it has also empowered 15,000+ professionals nationwide.

Our Mission

At AAFM® India, we aim to set global standards in Wealth & Finance education, equipping the professionals with top certifications like CWM®, AFA®, CH.E.®, CTEP®, and CFOM®—designed to enhance the skills, credibility, and career growth of our certificants.

Our Vision

At AAFM® India, we make quality financial education accessible to all no matter where they live or how old they are. Focused on real-world skills, we equip learners with the knowledge and confidence to grow, whether starting fresh or advancing in their careers.
